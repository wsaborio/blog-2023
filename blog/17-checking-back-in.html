17. Checking back in

2023-03-22

I haven’t been writing as much publicly as I do personally, across my Notes app, Google Docs, or in my notebook.

It’s startling to see that the last time I updated this blog was in … 2019! Holy cow!

I’d like to change that, as when I’ve written more in the past it’s kicked off great conversations with interesting people, clarified my thinking on topics I’ve found worth investigating, and generally increased the amount of serendipity in my life.

So, I’ll be writing a bit more in the coming weeks and months. I’ll keep things shorter and looser, to reduce the effort required.
